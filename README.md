# HR Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

## Overview

This project analyzes employee data to understand workforce trends related to attrition, overtime, salary hikes, promotions, business travel, and job levels.

## Dashboard Features

* Interactive slicers
* KPI cards
* Pivot charts
* Employee trend analysis
* Department-wise insights

## Key KPIs

* Total Employees
* Attrition Rate
* Employees Working Overtime
* Average Salary Hike
* Frequent Business Travelers

## Key Insights

* Research & Development has the highest overtime workload.
* Most employees belong to Job Levels 1 and 2.
* Attrition rate is 16.12%.
* Research & Development receives the highest average salary hike.
